# Mental Engineering 🧠

Welcome to **Mental Engineering**. This repository is a personal space where I document the systems, mindsets, and philosophies behind my growth, alongside my journey into software development and programming.

## 👤 About Me
I am 12 years old, and I decided to start building my future right now. I have no intention of waiting until adulthood to become productive or to map out my academic and career goals. 

## 🎯 Why This Repository?
Before writing a single line of Python code, I believe a great programmer needs to **refactor their own mind**. I am using this space to:
* Document my thoughts in English to practice and sharpen my language skills.
* Share my philosophy on productivity, happiness, and personal growth.
* Prepare the foundation for when I start uploading my first scripts and applications.

> "Ultimately, results are what truly matter."
